FIR REGISTRATION

 

1. Approach your nearest Police station in case of registration of FIR

  
 

2. Application should be addressed to SHO.

  
 

3. All the facts should be narrated in it.

  
  4. Give your address signatures and contact number at its end.  
5. You can contact Sub-Divisional Police officer or SP of your concerned area or SSP Islamabad in case your complaint is not redressed at Police station.
